I. Core Principles & Philosophy
Goal: Landfill reclamation, urban mining, and waste-to-resource conversion to implement a circular economy model.

Design: Modular, insect-like Swarm of task-specific units. This design prioritizes flexibility, redundancy, and scalability.

Objective: To recover all buried resources (high-value components, bulk materials, and energy) while managing and neutralizing environmental toxins.

Constraint: Maintain a Closed-Loop system, using recovered materials for internal repairs and new unit construction where possible.

II. The Swarm Units and Their Functions

Unit Name,Primary Function,Key Sub-Tasks,Safety / Triage Role
Scout Unit (The Mapper),"Large-area, non-contact surveying and 3D mapping.","Uses GPR, Hyperspectral Imaging, and Magnetometers to detect material veins, moisture, and density.","Generates a Digital Site Map (DSM), flagging dangerous Avoid Zones (hazardous hot spots, deep leachate)."
Snatcher Unit (The Retriever),"Targeted, high-precision retrieval based on the DSM.","Performs the Triage extraction of high-value, intact components (chips, sensors) and isolated hazardous materials.","Ensures delicate, high-risk items are removed before bulk mechanical stress."
Worm Unit (The Eater),Bulk material ingestion and primary sorting.,"Separates excavated material into four basic streams: Metals, Plastics, Organics, and Inert Materials.","Uses close-range sensors (e.g., in-line XRF/Optical) for final material verification on the conveyor."
Mover Unit (The Logistics),Material transport and supply chain management.,"A standardized, multi-purpose chassis designed to quickly exchange Specialized Vessels for delivery.",Minimizes cross-contamination by using dedicated vessels for each material stream.
