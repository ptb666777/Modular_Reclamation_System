Yes, this strategy is not only viable but essential for a complex environment like a landfill. It creates a three-tiered sensing and action hierarchy:

Macro-Level Mapping: A separate, fast unit (the "Scout") maps the area.

Micro-Level Sorting: The "Worm" unit focuses only on eating and separating.

Targeted Retrieval: A third unit (the "Snatcher") retrieves high-value items based on the map.

Current Capabilities in Surface Scanning
We have come very far in surface and sub-surface non-invasive scanning technologies that can create the kind of layered map you need.
